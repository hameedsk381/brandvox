# ReputationOS AI -- Strategic Implementation Plan (12-Month Roadmap)

## Vision

Transform ReputationOS AI from an **AI Review Management Platform** into
an **AI Brand Intelligence Platform** that helps businesses understand
**why growth is slowing and what actions will improve revenue**.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

# Strategic Principles

-   Sell outcomes, not features.
-   Focus on one customer segment before expanding.
-   Use AI to generate business intelligence, not just automate tasks.
-   Build a platform that connects customer feedback to business
    performance.

------------------------------------------------------------------------

### Deliverables

-   Google Business Profile integration

-   AI reply generation

-   Approval workflow

-   Multi-location support
-   Executive reports

-   Google account connected in \<5 minutes
